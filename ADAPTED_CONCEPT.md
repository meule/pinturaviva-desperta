## What Changed From The Original Brief

The original agency brief aimed at a futuristic product launch page. The actual video points in a more specific and stronger direction:

- not chrome sci-fi
- not fake hardware
- not plastic "future" aesthetics
- yes to premium craft, motion, and believable brand storytelling

The strongest version of the idea is:

`Pintura Viva` is the brand.
`DESPERTA` is the featured product release.
The promise is that painting feels alive, emotional, and in motion.

## Recommended Positioning

DESPERTA should be presented as a premium watercolor concept from the near future of creativity, not from the near future of gadgets.

Core positioning line:

`A living watercolor concept that wakes drawings into motion.`

Supporting brand line taken from the video:

`La vida comença amb moviment.`
